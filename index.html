ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Ash and I'm 15 years old. I'm in year 10 and go to Assumption College Kilmore. My hobbies and interests include drawing, web development/design and listening to music. The type of music I like is rock and metal. Artists/bands I recommend are Green Day, Bad Omens, These Four Walls and Nirvana. I would like to become a game artist/designer for a career as I find character design really interesting and would like to do it myself. I am also planning to do a game development VET next year.

Drawing of myself! I use Adobe Photoshop but I originally used Paint Tool SAI V2.0 as an art program :) I have been drawing digitally since 2016 and did mini animations for fun. Sadly I don't animate as much anymore but I want to get back into it.
