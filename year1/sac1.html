Industry Knowledge/Web Dev

For the first half of the SAC, we had to research, learn and discuss about the gaming and film/VFX industry. This section covers areas such as behaviour and ethics, industry trends, cost and budgets, job roles, trade union, hardware/software, gaming platforms, copyright and media law.

The second half of this SAC is what you’re using right now, my web portfolio. Throughout this powerpoint, it covers each step that was taken to create the website such as its wireframe sketches, final design in Adobe Illustrator feedback. It also includes extras such as scheduling and draw overs.
